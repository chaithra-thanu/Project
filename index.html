SAMSUNG

6000mah battery SAMOLED display. 48MP triple rear camera. New Exynr 9611 processer with the best prize. The Samsung Galaxy M30s measures 159.00 x 75.10 x 8.90mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 188.00 grams. It was launched in Opal Black, Pearl White, and Sapphire Blue colours.The Samsung Galaxy M30s runs One UI based on Android 9 Pie and packs 64GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 512GB) with a dedicated slot.

ONE PLUS

OnePlus 6 smartphone runs on Android v8.1 (Oreo) operating system. Phone is loaded with 6GB RAM, 64GB internal storage. The device performs well, looks good, has a decent display and good software. It's a decently rounded device overall and should work well for many mid-range users.

Apple

The iPhone 11 dimensions are 150.9mm x 75.7mm x 8.3mm (H x W x D). It weighs about 194 grams. Best-in-class performance. It features a 6.1-inch all-screen LCD display and is powered by Apple’s new A13 bionic chip with Third-Generation Neural Engine. The display is the company’s proprietary Liquid Retina HD display having Multi-Touch capability with IPS technology. Other special features of the display are True Tone, Wide colour (P3) and Haptic touch. It comes coated with a fingerprint resistant oleophobic coating.
